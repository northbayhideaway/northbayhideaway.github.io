Welcome

Welcome to North Bay Hideaway! North Bay Hideaway is a newly-built two bedroom, two bath rental home in the beautiful northwoods of Wisconsin. Although it is officially located in Danbury, it is specifically located in Webb Lake township, a popular destination with a nice variety of activities all within a short drive. It also is on the Webb Lake trail system! It is in Voyager Village with access to several lakes, hiking/biking trails, tennis, pickleball, indoor swimming pool, golf and much more! We hold a Wisconsin tourism rental license as well as a Burnett county short term rental permit. Plans are to have it available for rent mid-September through mid-May.
